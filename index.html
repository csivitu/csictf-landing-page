What is csictf?

csictf is a jeopardy-style capture the flag competition. You will be presented with cyber security questions on various categories like Pwn, Web, OSINT, Linux, Crypto, Forensics, and Reversing. Your goal is to solve these challenges and find and submit the flag on our website, to gain points on the leaderboard!

What if i'm a beginner?

That's perfect. csictf is targeted towards beginners, and most challenges will be geared towards helping you learn a wide range of cybsersec concepts, and at the same time, having fun!
